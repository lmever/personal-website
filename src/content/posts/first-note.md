This site is designed to stay small and easy to change. Notes are Markdown files, projects are Markdown files, and resume information lives in structured JSON.

That means the website can grow from a simple landing page into a durable archive without adopting a complex backend too early.

Future updates can add richer project pages, imported resume data, and longer essays while keeping the same foundation.
